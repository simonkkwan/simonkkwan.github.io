SMART YETI

2015 - UI/UX

PROJECT DETAILS

CLIENT: Smart Yeti

ROLE : UI/Visual Design, Branding

Pulseband is a concept healthcare wearable device that provides constant tracking of pulse and blood pressure and is marketed towards patients with heart and other health conditions (as well as those at risk of developing these conditions). My team at DESIGNATION Labs was tasked with creating a companion mobile app for the Pulseband, as well as a logo, branding, and a marketing site for the products.
